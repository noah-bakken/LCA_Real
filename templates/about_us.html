About Us

The Luddy Consulting Association is dedicated to empowering students with the knowledge, skills, and connections needed to navigate and excel in the consulting industry. We support tomorrow's leaders with consulting and tech education, developing innovative solutions to shape our community while exploring the dynamic world of technology consulting.

Our Goals

  • Provide resources and advice: We offer guidance based on past experiences to help members find internships and jobs related to consulting.
  • Build a supportive community: We foster an environment that guides members towards their career and professional goals for long-term success.
  • Focus on Technology Consulting: While we primarily focus on Technology Consulting, we also explore other practices within the consulting industry.
  • Offer practical opportunities: We provide real-life client projects, networking opportunities, skill-building workshops, and insider knowledge on acing case and behavioral interviews.
